Refugee Relocation through AI Mapping

With the purpose of using Artificial Intelligence to analyse safe routes for refugee relocation, I established the Award-Winning SafeR initiative.
The SafeR model aims to utilise the factors relevant to refugees' safety and assess optimal routes for their relocation based on danger zones, open borders, and available resources among other such information.
We have been awarded and backed by Intel with a 1500$ scholarship as one of the top 10 Global AI Impact Award winners.
Now partnered with Academic mentors and students from MIT, Yale, and Ashoka, we are currently researching the problems faced by refugees and improving on our initial concept.
